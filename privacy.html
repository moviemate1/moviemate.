Privacy

A short privacy note for MovieMate users.

MovieMate stores public suggestions, likes, and reviews so the website can display them to other visitors. If you choose to post a review, the name you enter can be visible publicly on the site.

Try not to post private personal information in comments or title suggestions. This website is intended for public movie and series discovery.

If you want stronger moderation, reporting, or account controls later, those can be added as the website grows.
